Plot

In 1936, American archaeologist Indiana Jones recovers a special gold idol from an ancient Peruvian temple.

A rival steals the idol and Jones makes his way back to America - to find that the Nazis are seeking the Ark of the Covenant

This movie follows Indiana's journey and travels as he tries to recover the ark.
